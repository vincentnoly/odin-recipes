Cucumber Lemonade

Description

A refreshing drink for any warm summer day

Ingredients

  • 1 cucmber, thinly sliced
  • 1 (12 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Steps

  1. Place the cucumber slives into a large pitcher.
  2. Pour the lemonade concentrate over the cucmber and let stand for 10 minutes.
  3. Pour in the lemon-lime soda and pour into glasses to serve.
